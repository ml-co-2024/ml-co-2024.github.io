ML and Constrained Optimization

Course Info#

This is the web site for the course “ML and Constrained Optimization”, within the PhD program in Computer Science and Engineering of the University of Bologna. The course will cover:

  • An overview of some Constrained Optimization methods (including both numeric and discrete optimization)
  • A presentation of selected methods to account for constraints in Machine Learning
  • A presentation of selected methods to integrate optimization and Machine Learning

Teaching Material#

Lectures will be given in hybrid mode, the physical and virtual room are reported each Google calendar event. As an alternative, you can also access the virtual room directly.

The teaching material will be made available my means of github repositories, which will be listed here as the course start approaches.
